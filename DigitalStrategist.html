Digital Marketing Strategist

A Digital Marketing Strategist is responsible for developing solutions to meet clients’ brand objectives based on consumer insight and data. Spotting trends and technologies that play roles in consumer’s lives, as well as familiarity with SEM/SEO strategies are beneficial skills to have in this position. The Digital Marketing Specialist will also help cross-functional teams develop and evolve ideas to bring together brand goals, retailer objectives and consumer needs.

Skills:

  • Marketing
  • Business Knowledge
  • Communication
  • Analytics
