CG Artist

A CG Artists work with the development or production of 3D or 2D world and models to video games. Normally a CG Artist works with finding the balance between artistic sensibilities and technical understanding in an effort to make a video game which works in accordance with an already planned game concept create by a development team.

Skills:

  • Story telling
  • Scripting
  • Sketching and Painting
  • Concept art
  • 2d animation
  • 3d modeling and 3d animation
  • Recording and video editing
  • Game integration
