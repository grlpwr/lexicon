Interaction Designer

Interaction designer helps to create meaningful relationships between people and the product and services they use. Understanding user journey and behaviors is crucial for them. They are part of design, marketing, development or creative team identifying key interactions of the product, creating prototypes and interfaces for applications, platforms and websites.

Skills:

  • Communication
  • User Flow
  • Documentation
  • Interactive Prototyping
  • Basic Coding
  • Wireframing
